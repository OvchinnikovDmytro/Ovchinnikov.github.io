Past Simple Test

1. What is the past simple form of the verb "play"?

a) played
b) play
c) plays

2. Which sentence is in the past simple tense?

a) She is playing soccer.
b) She played soccer yesterday.
c) She plays soccer every day.

3. What is the past simple form of the verb "study"?

a) studied
b) studying
c) studies

4. Which sentence is in the past simple tense?

a) They are watching a movie.
b) They watched a movie last night.
c) They watch movies often.

5. What is the past simple form of the verb "eat"?

a) ate
b) eaten
c) eating
