Present Continuous Test

1. What is she doing right now?

a) She drinks coffee.
b) She is drinking coffee.
c) She will drink coffee.

2. Where are they going?

a) They go to the park.
b) They are going to the park.
c) They went to the park.

3. Why is he laughing?

a) He laughs at the joke.
b) He is laughing at the joke.
c) He will laugh at the joke.

4. What is she saying?

a) She says hello.
b) She is saying hello.
c) She said hello.

5. Who is playing the piano?

a) He plays the piano.
b) He is playing the piano.
c) He played the piano.
