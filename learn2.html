Present Simple Test

1. What is the third person singular form of the verb "to go"?

a) goes
b) go
c) going

2. Which sentence is in the present simple tense?

a) She is going to the store.
b) She goes to the store every day.
c) She went to the store yesterday.

3. What is the present simple form of the verb "to eat"?

a) eats
b) eating
c) ate

4. Which sentence is in the present simple tense?

a) They are watching a movie.
b) They watch movies every weekend.
c) They watched a movie last night.

5. What is the present simple form of the verb "to write"?

a) writes
b) writing
c) wrote
