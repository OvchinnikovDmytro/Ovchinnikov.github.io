Present Simple Test

1. What will you do tomorrow?

a) I'm going to sleep.
b) I'll sleep.
c) I sleep.

2. Where will they travel next summer?

a) They're going to travel to Italy.
b) They'll travel to Italy.
c) They travel to Italy.

3. Who will win the game?

a) They win the game.
b) They'll win the game.
c) They're going to win the game.

4. What will she buy at the store?

a) She's going to buy groceries.
b) She'll buy groceries.
c) She buys groceries.

5. How will you celebrate your birthday?

a) I celebrate with my friends.
b) I'm going to celebrate with my friends.
c) I'll celebrate with my friends.
